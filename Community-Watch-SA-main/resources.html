General Safety Tips

Home Safety

  • Ensure all doors and windows are securely locked, even when home.
  • Install alarm systems and security cameras.
  • Keep valuables out of sight from windows.
  • Be cautious of strangers at your door.
  • Regularly check your perimeter for vulnerabilities.

Personal Safety

  • Be aware of your surroundings, especially in public spaces.
  • Avoid walking alone at night in dimly lit areas.
  • Limit distractions like cell phone use when walking.
  • Share your travel plans with a trusted person.
  • Carry a personal alarm if you feel unsafe.

Vehicle Safety

  • Always lock your car doors and close windows, even for short stops.
  • Avoid leaving valuables visible in your car.
  • Park in well-lit, secure areas.
  • Be wary of people approaching your vehicle at intersections.
