About Community Safety SA

Welcome to Community Safety SA, a platform dedicated to fostering a safer environment for all residents of South Africa. Our mission is to empower communities through information, resources, and collaborative action.

Our Vision

To create a South Africa where every individual feels secure and has the knowledge and tools to contribute to their own safety and the safety of their neighbours.

What We Do

  • Provide accessible safety tips and crime prevention strategies.
  • Offer a straightforward channel for reporting non-urgent incidents.
  • Aggregate and present key emergency contact information.
  • Facilitate awareness of local community safety initiatives.

Our Commitment

We are committed to building a robust and reliable online resource that supports community-led safety efforts. While this platform serves as an information and reporting hub, we always emphasize that immediate emergencies require direct contact with the relevant authorities (e.g., SAPS, Ambulance, Fire Department).

Contact Us

For general inquiries or feedback about this website, please email us at: info@communitysafetysa.org

Please note: Do NOT use this email for emergency incident reporting. Use the Report Incident form for non-urgent matters, or dial 10111 for emergencies.
